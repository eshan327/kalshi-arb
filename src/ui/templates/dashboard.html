Model, Execution, and Runtime

P(model) is the pricing model probability, while P(book) is short-horizon orderbook pressure. Execution runtime panels reflect observe, paper, or live mode behavior.

Read me — what P(model) and P(book) mean

P(model) estimates the probability that the official final-minute settlement average resolves above strike. P(book) summarizes near-term orderbook pressure (OBI, TFI, MPP) and is used as execution confirmation.

Green means probability above 50% (YES skew), red means below 50% (NO skew), and gray means neutral or unavailable.
